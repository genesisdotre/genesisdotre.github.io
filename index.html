• Regenerative culture, systematic change
• Liquid democracy, democracy.earth

• Effective altruism, Triple bottom line

Rainforest restoration

In many places jungle was cut down. Planting bamboo that enriches the soil, captures CO2 and is used to create construction material plus generates clean, renewable energy from biomass.
